How do
we design

We follow a tried and tested process to ensure that our clients are completely satisfied with the end result.

Step 1

Consultation and on-site review

We start by conducting consultation with our clients to understand their vision and requirements. We then conduct an on-site review to assess the space and identify any challenges that may need to be addressed.

Step 2

Letter of agreement

Once we have a clear understanding of the project scope, we prepare a letter of agreement that outlines all the details and sets expectations for both parties.

Step 3

Design and development

Our team of designers gets to work on creating a design that is tailored to the client's needs and preferences. We provide detailed 3D models to help clients visualize the end result.

Step 4

Construction and installation

Once the design is approved, we move on to construction and installation. Our team work diligently to ensure that the project is completed on time and within budget and our clients are completely satisfied with the end result.
